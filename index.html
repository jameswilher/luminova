How this works

The switch swaps two product photos.

To make every item look like this, prepare two matching images for each product: one OFF and one ON. They must use the same angle, crop, product position, background, and shadows. Then the switch simply changes which image is visible.

Shopify ready concept

This is the exact interaction you can build in Shopify.

In Shopify, upload the OFF and ON image pair for each product, then connect them to product metafields or custom product card code.
